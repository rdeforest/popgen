Pop Ballad Generator!

This is an experimental music generator I made in my spare time. It follows a very common chord progression, (I,V,vi,IV), and generates random notes within the key and up to a certain range of octaves. Let me know what you think! jsat@outlook.com

Future work: New chord progressions, octave ranges below base key, time signatures, pauses/unslurred notes
